About BinaryBotanic

I’m a coder, dog‑parent, and solarpunk enthusiast who spends evenings building thriving ecosystems—whether that’s a bustling city in Planet Zoo or a clean, responsive UI for a client’s web app. My Swedish roots give me a warm, slightly accented way of speaking, and I’ve turned that charm into a signature vibe during streams and tutorials.

When I’m not tweaking CSS or debugging JavaScript, you’ll find me sipping oat‑milk lattes, tending to my houseplants, or exploring the latest eco‑tech gadgets. All of that energy fuels the cozy, down‑to‑earth vibe you’ll see across my designs, videos, and community spaces.
